Sentiment Analysis

Introduction

Sentiment analysis, or opinion mining, is a growing NLP and data analytics field that extracts insights and emotions from text. It helps gauge public sentiment about topics, products, or events, and this project explores its methods, applications, and impact across domains. Sentiment analysis has gained remarkable prominence in recent years, driven by the exponential growth of digital content, social media, and online communication. The advent of web platforms, user-generated content, and the proliferation of reviews, comments, and social media posts have created vast repositories of textual data.

Sentiment Definition

Sentiment analysis, also known as opinion mining, is a natural language processing technique that involves the systematic extraction of sentiments, attitudes, and emotional tone from textual data. It is used to determine whether a piece of text conveys a positive, negative, or neutral sentiment, making it a valuable tool for understanding and analyzing public opinions, customer feedback, and social media content.
